Reduce Plastic Waste and Make the World a Better Place

  • Posted on 7 May, 2021
  • Environment
  • By Zahra

Plastic pollution has become one of the most urgent environmental issues. Since Plastics are inexpensive and durable, the production of plastics continues to increase over time. The increasing number of plastics will overwhelm the world’s ability to decompose and eventually lead to huge issues of piling plastic waste. As we all already know, plastics are very convenient to use and have become our basic utility in our daily lives. Start from water bottles (PET), plastic packaging (HDPE and LDPE), single-use face masks (PP), cups (PS), and others. The ones that I mentioned earlier are only several products out of a million unmentioned products that are also made from plastic.

Those products have their own lifespan, yet they all share the same similarities, which is, taking so long to decompose. It is safe to say that plastic material may persist in the environment forever since it decomposes only after hundreds of years. And why is that? It’s because plastics don’t originally exist in nature, it is manmade. This means that there are no naturally occurring organisms that can break them down effectively or at all. Take a look at the bigger picture, when we compare the life span to its decomposition rate, we can conclude that plastic waste can easily pile up if we don’t make any move.

Now, let’s take a look at our country Indonesia, should we start to take this issue as a huge problem? Yes. Researchers show Indonesia and India ranked highest on which countries plastic waste ends up in the oceans. It is not something to be proud of, we have to make a change even if it’s very small, it starts from us and we have to raise our awareness toward this issue. Firstly, we should know about the effect of plastic waste on our surroundings, the oceans are the most affected by plastic pollution since most plastic waste ends up in the ocean. Whether it’s from irresponsible people who throw trash in the nearest river or plastic debris from the runoff.

There are an estimated 270,000 tons of plastic floating through the world’s seas where it threatens 700 marine species with its presence. Further, there is growing evidence that plastics play a role in rising rates of species extinctions. Many sea creatures eat plastics and end up dying. Knowing these facts, we shall spread awareness and encourage people around us to make a change little by little. Today, our convenience stores such as Indomaret and Alfamart don't provide plastic bags and change them to reusable paper bag. Several restaurants also stop providing straw to decrease the amount of plastic.

As a part of the young generation, I believe that we should be the ones who initiate the movement. If it’s not us then who, we have to spread awareness and try our best to live a zero-waste of living in order to make the world a better place. We can start by bringing our own Tumblr, use a paper bag, and throw our trash in its appropriate place a.k.a trash bin. We should also reconsider buying small packaged products and buy bulk food instead to decrease the use of plastic on food packaging. Other than that, we can use our own social media platform to campaign on raising awareness regarding plastic waste. Let’s spread awareness by sharing information on the effect of plastic waste on our loved ones and make them join our movement on reducing the use of plastic waste! Reduce plastic, less pollution, better place to live in! Reduce plastic waste and make the world a better place!
