About Us

We will tell you who we are

Hello, 11Bers here! We are one of the two science classes in Madania School Academic Year 2020/2021 with Sir Budi Purwanto and Sir Sharif Maulana as our base teachers.

We proudly present our magazine, BUZZINE! This magazine website is an assignment from our English Language class directed by Miss Yuyun. There is a big possibility that it will be our last project together as a class, so we decided to go all out! For the magazine’s theme, we chose ‘2021’ because even though it hasn't been long since the year began, a lot has happened. That is why we provide you with the latest news and issues in the year of 2021, ranging from COVID-19, sport, technology, environment, and many more. Enjoy various content to keep you informed and up to date. With our magazine, look back to the past events and prepare yourself on what’s to come in the future!
